Our Products

Innovatively designed, our products offer time-saving solutions that take the fuss out of day-to-day life. Built to take on the rigors of modern living, our quality products have reliability at their heart.

Why Choose Us?

Pifco is a way of life and with our vast range of versatile products spanning cleaning, cooking and heating, our products boast some of the most advanced technologies offering unrivalled quality and value for money.

100 Years of Innovation

Established in Manchester in 1900, Provincial Incandescent Fittings Co or PIFCO as they are now better known, sold a wide range of lighting, stoves & household goods. From the first oil filled heaters, to developments in lighting and domestic appliances PIFCO have been experts in electricals for over 100 years.

Today PIFCO is a household name, trusted by millions to provide cleaning, heating and lighting solutions to the nation. PIFCO products have a place in the heart and history of UK domestic appliances. With a reputation based on quality, value and innovation. Paving the way with time saving products for over 100 years.
